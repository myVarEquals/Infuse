Custom Essential Oil Blend

100% Pure & Natural

Essential Oils are the natural essences of plants acquired by steam distillation or cold-expression from a single botanical source. At Infuse, we offer a wide selection of pure, unadulterated Essential Oils obtained from the blossoms, leaves, bark and roots of flowers, herbs, fruits and spices. We offer a large selection of Certified Organic, 100% Pure and Therapeutic Grade Essential Oils, and we offer Absolutes that are rare and exotic in 3% dilutions. Diluted in Golden Jojoba, 3% dilutions are the most affordable way to recognize and utilize the beauty of these oils. Our oils are purchased from one supplier who is dedicated to providing the highest quality pure essential oils.

At Infuse, feel free to come create your custom scent to INFUSE into one of our many bases, or simply buy a 10ml bottle of your unique blend.
