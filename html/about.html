Who is Infuse?

I

n 2015, at the age of 33, a south Philadelphia native now living in south N.J., a wife and mother of a three year old daughter, I found myself dealing with heart issues and a very scary second pregnancy, I was placed in the predicament of needing to take life saving cardiac medications while pregnant and for the rest of my life. Barely 27 weeks pregnant and knowing that our lives depended on this medicine, I willingly took my prescribed medication, but wondered about what chemicals I was unwillingly accepting into my body.

Around the same time our then 3 year old daughter started using bath bombs. It was when our caramel skin colored baby girl was dyed hot pink after soaking in a popular bath bomb, that I started researching ingredients and looking for a more natural and safer alternatives.

I decided to buy some ingredients and test a few batches to make at home. My first batch was a success and were used as Christmas gifts! Paired with my husbands ever growing need to perfect a process, the idea of Infuse was born. We were sick of having to walk into an over bearing scented store and then having to choose between what scents the stores think we want. Instead, we wanted to walk into a store and make what scent we wanted. Like an ice cream parlour for bath bombs. "I want those sprinkles, that avocado body butter, my custom blend of wake up sunshine essential oils, and yes the activated charcoal please..."

The thought persisted beyond what was in those bath bombs and now "What is in this shampoo? What is in this lotion?" While the mad scientist kept improving the process, and sourcing the best raw ingredients, and natural and organic bases made in the USA, we have now complied a whole family of diverse products to be infused with your custom scent.
