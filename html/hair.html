Conditioner, Daily Use

Base is: Vegan Friendly & Gluten Free

Base made with 89% Certified Organic Ingredients

Our daily use hair conditioner is made from naturally derived ingredients, and several organic oils. Gentle enough to use every day, this base is loaded with volume-izing ingredients which are sure to leave your hair soft and healthy looking. ​ Many people ask us if this will work as a 'leave in' conditioner? Yes, it will for most hair types. Those with thin, fine hair probably should use this as a normal wash out product, but for all other hair types, it can be left in. Those who have tested it as both a leave-in and a wash-out conditioner, rave for its ability to soften the hair without weighing it down. As a leave in: just remember a little will go a long way! Even better yet, we've tried this as an all over body lotion - and its simply fabulous for that as well!

* Aloe Barbadensis (aloe) Juice, * Orbygnia oleifera (babassu) oil, behentrimonium methosulfate, cetearyl alcohol, * Olea Europaea (extra virgin olive) oil, * Prunus Armeniaca (apricot kernel) oil, * glycerin, glyceryl stearate, sodium stearoyl lactylate, Anthemis nobilis (chamomile) extract, panthenol DL, Vitamin E acetate, rosemary seed extract, vitamin C, vitamin B3, vitamin B5, vitamin B6, propanediol (naturally derived preservative), ethylexyl glycerin (naturally derived preservative), potassium sorbate (food grade preservative). (*) denotes a certified organic ingredient.

We are often asked what is behentrimomium methosulfate? The name does sound daunting! Its common name of 'BTMS' is a wax that is derived from the Colza plant. Our BTMS holds a green rating on the Skin Deep Comsetic Safety Database, and despite the name it isn't a sulfate ingredient at all. Its super mild, and imparts body and texture to your hair.
